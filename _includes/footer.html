What's this about?

This is the Batang-X Universe, a serial web novel. Batang-X is a superhero from the Philippines, and since the original IP holders decided not to renew his trademark, putting him in public domain, I ( GaiusCoffee) decided to reboot his story. A "serial web novel" is a just a fancy way of saying that I will update this story periodically, in this website. I hope you guys enjoy, and feel free to contact me regarding your thoughts and comments on the story.
